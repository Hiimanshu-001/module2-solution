Itachi Uchiha:

People live their lives bound by what they accept as correct and true. That's how they define ‘reality’. But what does it mean to be ‘correct’ or ‘true’? Merely vague concepts... Their ‘reality’ may all be a mirage. Can we consider them to simply be living in their own world, shaped by their beliefs?

No matter how devastated you may be by your own weakness or uselessness, set your heart ablaze. Grit your teeth and look straight ahead. Even if you stop and crouch down, time won’t wait for you or snuggle you and grieve along with you. Don’t feel bad that I’m going to die here. As a Hashira, of course I would shield my juniors. I must do what is my duty. I won’t let anyone know what’s here.
